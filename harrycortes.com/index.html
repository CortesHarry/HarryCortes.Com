What is harrycortes.com?

harrycortes.com is a small website that I started as an educational project. I had minimal knowledge of scripting and web design, and that was not acceptable. harrycortes.com quickly became much more than a teacher: it became a project. A project that I felt worthy of showing. I expanded on it; styled it simple, yet regal. I eventually launched harrycortes.com during the Fall of 2017.

Currently, my goal for harrycortes.com is to better showcase myself to the world around me.
